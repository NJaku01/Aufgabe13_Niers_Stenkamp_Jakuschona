Here all routes will be shown on the map, which have been added with the (Route-Editor). For each meeting of an userRoute with an other userRoute or animalRoute there is an intersection marked on the map.
By the filter below you can manage which of the routes and intersections you want to be shown.

User_ID: Animal: IntersectionIDS:
Show User Routes
Show Animal Routes
Show Intersections with user
Show Intersections with Animals

If Intersections are shown, also the corresponding routes are shown no matter if the buttons for the routes are checked. If you want to see a specific User Route/ Animal Route/ Intersection you can input the relative ID and regulate by the checkboxes what you want to see corresponding to your input.
Proof if you entered the userID the right way!
- To show all routes, just leave the field empty
- To show routes of just one user enter only the userID
- To show routes of more users disconnect the userIDs by ";" e.g. "1; 2"
